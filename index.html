What Is Immunization Tracker?

Immunization Tracker is an application that helps parents and healthcare providers work together to ensure that children the vaccinations they need to stay protected and healthy throughout childhood.

Supporting Parents

With an Immunization Tracker account, you can track the immunization records for all of your children in one place and access them wherever you go - Which vaccinations they have received as well as the ones they still need. Whether you are using your family doctor, an urgent care facility or a pharmacy it is easy to grant access to any medical professional so they can officially update your child's records.

Empowering Providiers

Using Immunization Tracker, healthcare providers can easily view the immunization history of all of their patients and identify anyone that may have missed an important vaccination. Even if patients visit multiple medical professionals, Immunization Tracker shows the complete record.
